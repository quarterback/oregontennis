Selection Criteria

How All-State Teams Are Selected

Our All-State selections are based entirely on state tournament results. No voting, no politics - just objective recognition of tournament performance.

2025 Updates

  • First Team: Now limited to top 3 (Champion, Runner-up, 3rd place) - making it truly elite
  • Second Team: 4th place, consolation champion, semifinalists
  • Third Team: NEW - Consolation runner-up, quarterfinalists
  • District Player of the Year: NEW - Season-long excellence in each district
  • State Newcomer of the Year: NEW - Outstanding 9th graders

Why Tournament Results?

Given Oregon's size, most teams don't play outside their district during the regular season. Using state tournament results ensures fair recognition across all regions and eliminates subjective bias.
